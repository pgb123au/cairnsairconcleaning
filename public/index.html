Why a Cairns specialist

Three things every Cairns aircon clean should get right.

Tropical mould is the killer issue — not dust.

Southern-state aircon technicians treat a clean as a dust-and-debris job. In Cairns that misses 80% of what’s going on. The wet-tropics climate sits at 25–30°C with 70–95% humidity for most of the year — perfect breeding conditions for Cladosporium and Aspergillus, the two tropical mould species that colonise the evaporator coil and drain pan. They form a black biofilm you can’t see from outside, release spores back into the room every time the fan kicks on, and cause the classic sour-musty smell. A proper Cairns clean has to chemically treat the coil and biocide the drain pan — not just rinse the filter.

Coastal salt-air corrosion on the outdoor compressor.

Northern beaches suburbs — Trinity Beach, Yorkeys Knob, Holloways Beach — sit metres from open ocean, with onshore breezes carrying chlorides directly onto the outdoor compressor. Within 3–4 years the aluminium fin pack of an untreated condenser will show visible salt corrosion; within 7 it’s leaking refrigerant through a pinhole. Standard service includes a condenser inspection, fin-pack rinse with a coil-safe cleaner, and a pressure test to catch slow leaks before the compressor burns out. Coastal units often need 6-monthly service, not annual.

Manufacturer warranty depends on documented annual service.

Daikin, Mitsubishi Electric and Fujitsu — the three brands that cover ~80% of Cairns installations — all require documented annual servicing by a qualified technician to maintain the 5-year compressor warranty. We’ve seen warranty claims rejected on 3-year-old units because the owner couldn’t produce a single service invoice. Every clean we do is logged with a dated, signed service report you can hand to the manufacturer with the warranty claim. ARCtick licence number on every report.

How it works

From booking to clean report — 5 stages.

1

Booking

Phone or online quote request. Most cleans within the week. Same/next-day for body-corp or commercial emergencies (mould smell, dripping unit, not cooling at all).

2

On-site

60–90 min per indoor split, half-day for ducted. Cover the room, full strip-down of the head unit, chemical coil clean, biocide drain pan, refit and run a temperature differential test.

3

Refrigerant check

Gauge set on the service port, pressure check against ambient. If pressure is low we leak-test with UV dye + nitrogen and quote an ARCtick recharge separately. R-32 or R-410A as required.

4

Service report

Dated, signed service report with before/after photos, temperature differential reading, refrigerant pressure, and ARCtick licence number. Goes to your inbox for the manufacturer warranty file.

5

Annual reminder

We diary your next service 12 months out and send a reminder. Most clients sign onto an annual plan after the first clean — cheaper and locks in the warranty paperwork.
