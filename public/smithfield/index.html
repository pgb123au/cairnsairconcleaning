Why Smithfield

The Smithfield aircon story.

New estate stock = new warranty paperwork.

The Cattana Road and Cairns Western Arterial land releases have delivered hundreds of new homes since 2018, most with a multi-head split or zoned ducted system installed by the builder. The handover contract usually includes a 5-year compressor warranty — but that warranty only stays valid with documented annual servicing by a qualified technician. We’ve seen warranty claims rejected in year 3 because the owner couldn’t produce a single service invoice. The first service we do on a new-build install establishes the warranty paperwork trail; the annual plan keeps it alive.

JCU student rentals = hard-run units.

Smithfield has a large JCU student-rental concentration along McGregor Road and the Smithfield Shopping Centre precinct — group-share houses where the aircon runs 24/7 through wet season, tenants change every 6–12 months, and nobody owns the maintenance relationship. Landlords are increasingly engaging us on annual end-of-tenancy cleans, which protects the unit, removes mould smell before re-letting, and keeps the warranty paperwork intact for eventual sale.

Ducted systems with poor return-air access.

Many 2000s–2010s Smithfield ducted installs have the return-air filter mounted in the ceiling of a hallway or laundry, where it gets forgotten for years. We’ve pulled return-air filters that were last changed at install — matted with dust to the point that the fan was struggling and the evaporator coil was running cold enough to ice up. A full ducted service includes replacement of the return-air filter every visit, plus a duct vacuum and a roof-cavity coil inspection.

Body-corp townhouse complexes.

The Smithfield townhouse and small-strata complexes off Yorkeys Knob Road, Faculty Close, and the Cattana Road developments all have common-property considerations — outdoor compressors on shared walls, condensate drains tied into stormwater, common-area body-corp maintenance budgets. We run whole-complex service days on a body-corp contract with consolidated invoicing and per-unit service reports for the owners’ warranty files.
