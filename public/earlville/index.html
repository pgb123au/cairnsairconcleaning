Why Earlville

The Earlville aircon story.

Body-corp split-system stock everywhere.

Earlville has one of the higher densities of small-strata residential complexes in Cairns — the 4 to 24-unit blocks along Mulgrave Road, Sheridan Street, the streets around the Stockland centre, and off Bayview Heights Road. Most run individual splits in each apartment with the outdoor compressors mounted on common-property walls or rooftops. The coordination problem (every unit needs the same service window) is exactly why owner-occupiers and rental owners skip the annual clean — we run whole-complex service days to fix that.

Stockland precinct commercial aircon.

The Stockland Earlville shopping centre and the surrounding strip-retail along Mulgrave Road run heavy commercial aircon — rooftop packages, cassette splits in shop-fit-outs, ducted condensers in older tenancies. We service the smaller tenants — pharmacies, cafes, dental and medical fit-outs, allied-health rooms — on quarterly or 6-monthly contracts. Commercial aircon failure in summer Cairns isn’t an inconvenience, it’s a trading-stoppage event.

Older 70s–90s family-home stock.

The Mulgrave Road and Pease Street family-home stretches are 70s through 90s brick-veneer and low-set timber builds, mostly with retrofitted single-head splits in main rooms. Many have been running 15+ years without proper service — we routinely find visible mould on the coil and a refrigerant pressure 15–20% below spec. A full deep clean and ARCtick top-up usually restores cooling output and adds another decade of life.

Rental property turnover.

Earlville has high rental turnover — tenants change every 12–24 months, often with the aircon never serviced between tenancies. We do a lot of end-of-tenancy and start-of-tenancy cleans for property managers, which removes mould smell complaints, protects the landlord’s warranty, and keeps the bond inspection clean.
